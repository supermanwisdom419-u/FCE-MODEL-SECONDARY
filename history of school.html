BRIEF HISTORY OF THE SCHOOL

The shool was establish in september 2002/2003 session by the proprietor, the provost Dr.i.oAkinmoyewa. The current provost is academic session commenced on 2nd october 2002 with thirty six (36) student compprising of jss1 with twenty four (24) student and twelve(12) in jss2.

The good leadership quality has led the school to experience different levels of achievement. In order to re-strategize the school for educational excellence, the school moved to the permanet site on 1st november, 2007.The school is enriched with highly qualified,dilligent and commited members of the staff. There are facilities such as well equipped laboratories for practical based subjects,Good transportation system ,medical personell,borehole,sporting equipment and library to mention but a few.

The school has turned out high number of students in WASSCE/NECO with 100% passes in almost all the subject over the years which has earned them admission in reputable instition in Nigeria and Diaspora. At the just concluded JAMB examination all the 83 students presented for the exam scored above 200. The highest score 335 was the fifth best in the country while seven others scored between 334 and 304. The students have demonstrated excellence in all ramifications with numerous awards to their credit.

The school currently runs a boarding house where students are given utmost parental care;indeed it is a home away from home and a second home indeed. The school adopts a management committee system which leads to change in power at the expiration of each tenure. The school management bord in currently headed by Dr.T.H.Adeniji, a chief lecturer in school of secondary school education (science), F.C.E Oseiele, Abeokuta while the current principal is Mr.Mofikoya J.A.
