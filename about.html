About Us

FCE Model Secondary School is a leading educational institution established in 2002/2003 under the Federal College of Education. We are committed to providing world-class secondary education that combines academic excellence with moral values.

Our Mission

  • To raise self-indepensent and reliable individuals
  • To encourage the maximization of God-given potential in the student
  • To build a strong bedrock upon which is future of nigeria really depends
  • To provide an all round development for all student
  • To serve as demonstration field for the experimentation of acquired skills and knoeledge for the main stream (F.C.E Abeokuta)

Our Core Values

  • Academic Excellence
  • Integrity and Discipline
  • Creativity and Innovation
  • Respect and Teamwork
  • Leadership Development
  • Neatness
  • Club Activities
  • Sport Activities
  • Healthy Life

our vision

  • To raise the potential leaders through academic excellence and moral uprightness.

Founded to serve the educational needs of staff and the surrounding community, the school has grown from 85 students to over 1,200 students today, with outstanding WAEC and NECO results every year.
